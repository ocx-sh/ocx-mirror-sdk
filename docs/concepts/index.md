# Concepts

Before you write a generator, three ideas worth holding in your head:

- **[The `url_index` format](url-index.md)** — what your generator produces and
  who consumes it.
- **[Generator flow](generators.md)** — how the SDK, your script, and the
  `ocx-mirror` binary fit together.
- **[Caching model](caching-model.md)** — what gets cached, for how long,
  and where.
